⚛️ About Quantum Quirks

Welcome to Quantum Quirks — a chaotic, colourful, and completely crazy journey through the weirdest corners of physics. Our mission? To teach you quantum mechanics without melting your brain into Schrödinger soup. 🍜

🌌 The Backstory

Once upon a quantum fluctuation, a passionate human named Liam decided that physics didn't need to be boring, scary, or written in hieroglyphics only decipherable by Nobel Prize winners. So he did what any rational person would do: built an entire platform from scratch, styled it with Tailwind CSS, sprinkled animated particles across spacetimes, and brought an army of AI-powered tutor bots into existence.

👩‍🚀 The Team

Meet the elite team behind Quantum Quarks:

  • Liam — Founder, developer, quantum dreamer, gradient enthusiast, accidental particle summoner.
  • EntangleBot™ — AI tutor specialising in confusing metaphors and spooky action at a distance.
  • DecoherenceBot™ — Dramatic, emotionally unstable, probably in a superposition of productivity and chaos.
  • MathBot™ — Would love to teach you math, but will go off on wild tangent derivations no one asked for.

💬 Final Thoughts

Whether you're a curious student, a physics lover, or someone who just wonders what in the universe a qubit is — you're welcome here. Quantum Quirks is a place to explore, laugh, and learn — with zero pressure a maximum chaos.

P.S. This page may or may not have been written by Liam and three over-caffeinated subatomic particles. ✍️
